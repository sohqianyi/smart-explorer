Why Us?


- We have the technical expertise of turning e-waste into useful refurbished products, with more than 15 years of experience. Refer to Recycled/Refurbished Results.

- We strive for sustainability and minimizing environmental contamination

- We focus on converting e-waste into reusable products

Our Mission


Convert

We aim to convert large proportions of e-wastes collected into reusable products such as refurbished desktops, laptops, monitors, projectors etc.

Save the Environment

A little effort goes a long way. We aim to help save the world environment with our efforts, by raising awareness of the importance of recycling, and creating a better environment for future generations to come.

Affordable

We aim to benefit people unable to afford new electronic products by refurbishing, upgrading and selling recycled products at an affordable price. Refer to our Donation Program.
