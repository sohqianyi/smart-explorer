About Us


Smart Explorer Enterprise, based in Singapore, was set up in 2004. We have an e-waste licence issued by NEA (National Environment Agency). We collect electronic wastes all around Singapore with the aim to convert as much of these wastes into reusable items.

Our Mission


Convert

We aim to convert large proportions of e-wastes collected into reusable products such as refurbished desktops, laptops, monitors, projectors etc.

Save the Environment

A little effort goes a long way. We aim to help save the world environment with our efforts, by raising awareness of the importance of recycling, and creating a better environment for future generations to come.

Affordable

We aim to benefit people unable to afford new electronic products by refurbishing, upgrading and selling recycled products at an affordable price.
